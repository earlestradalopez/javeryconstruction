Health and Safety Program

Occupational safety and health is a matter of great concern to the company, but it is ever greater concern to the employees, for the reason, javery constructions services believed that the project employee should share in the work of accident prevention. Attitudes have much to do with the employee’s safety performance. Attitude grows and like that grows, they flourish best thru continues safety training and monitoring. With this javery constructions comes up with the program to establish a culture of occupational health and safety among our project employees to support our customers zero accident program. Our goal is to develop an environment, safety and health culture not only in the work place but also in our own household.

Incident / Accident Invetigation

All accident has both indirect and direct causes. They may associate with unsafe working practices, inadequate supervision and training human error, poor machinery specification and other causes.

  • If accident, emergency situation shall be reported directly to the project safety supervisor within 24 hours.
  • All accident should have and proper documents and conducted proper investigation.
  • Fatality, death at site premises should be reported to safety manager within 3 days after occurrence of incident.
  • All accident shall report every 20th of the two BWC Dole.
  • All accident shall be recorded and documented.
  • Findings shall be corrected there after immediately.
  • In the event of less serious injury or potential life threatening dangerous occurrence, work stoppage in the area involved until imminent danger vanish. Protection of the general public within the vicinity of the construction:
  • Place barricades and warning signs.
  • Detail watchman and security to warn the public
  • Adequate lighting must be provided to assure safety warning
  • Install warning sign and caution tapes signage’s at cistern tank. Environmental control and protection:
  • Provision of waste disposal and garbage vain
  • Disposal of excess materials water proofing.
  • Disposal of chemical and empty pails

Personal Protective Equipment:

All workers shall require wearing personal protective equipment at all times. Safety head shall ensure that all workers and staff under his supervision are trained in the proper usages. Safety staff shall not allow or permit workers and staff to work with out or unless required safety equipment is used and worn properly.

  • Eye and Face Protection – prevent from entering foreign body into eye, prevent also in major eye injuries.
  • Safety Glasses – shield against frontal impact from hazard like flying object.
  • Goggles – eye protection from foreign body
  • Safety shoes – are design to protect for extremely hazardous, protect against water,chemical,nailed,oil and grease
  • Hardhat – protect from head falling materials.
  • All other personnal protective equipment that is need on the project site based on particular job assignment.
